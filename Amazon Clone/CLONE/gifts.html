Frequently Asked Questions

What can Amazon Gift Cards be redeemed towards?

Amazon Gift Cards are redeemable towards the purchase of millions of eligible goods and services provided by Amazon.com or its affiliates on Amazon.com, or certain of its affiliated properties, such as smile.amazon.com. Eligible goods and services are subject to change in our sole discretion.

Are there any shipping costs on Amazon Gift Cards?

Physical Amazon Gift Cards (except Anytime Gift Cards) are delivered with FREE One-Day shipping when you select One-Day shipping at checkout.

Are there any fees or expiration dates to use Amazon Gift Cards?

Amazon Gift Cards have no fees and no expiration date.

Someone has asked me to pay them with a gift card. Is this a scam?

Be wary of anyone who asks you to pay with a gift card, as this is often a scam. Never use Amazon.com Gift Cards for personal use outside of Amazon or its affiliated stores, and never use gift cards for other retailers and brands outside of their stores. Please visit this page for information if you think you have been the target of a gift card scam.
